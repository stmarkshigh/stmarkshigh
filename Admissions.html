ADMISSIONS

Secondary Admissions

For our excellent grade 7 students to apply for a space in one of our day or bording spaces, they are requested to bring their grade 7 results to the school and submit them to the Sincobile Hall where you shall find our dedicated staff of Deputies and Head of Departments who shall make a decision based on the academic performance of the child.

This normally happens during the first to second week of January. So if you miss this slot your application will not be considered.

High School

By the the time our student have reached High School, they will be fully equipped with the knowledge and skills to help them in choosing subjects that are inline with the careers they wish to persue in their lives. This is all thanks to the fact that our students are exposed to all the different subjects in different faculties of work, which enables them to eventually choose what they are interested in and what subjects must be chosen for that particular career.

AS & A Levels

To be accepted into our AS & A Level program, students are required to come to the school to sit for an entrance exam that will consist of:


  1. Mathematics
  2. Physical Science
  3. Accounts
  4. English
  5. Biology

Please note that students can only sit for the subjects they want to do for the AS & A level course. If students are accepted into the program, they will need to do a minimum of 4 subjects for the AS level of their studies. For those students who will choose to do the full 2 year course, they will be required to do a minimum of 3 science's to complete the A Level course.

Student can also apply with their IGCSE/EGCSE/Matric results and must have obtained at least a B Grade in each of the subjects he/she wishes to study at AS/A Level.

School Fees

School fees are paid in two instalments; the first installment must be paid in the first month of the year, January. The balance must be paid by the end of April. This means that all outstanding fees must be settled before this deadline.
