Subjects We Offer

St.Marks offers a wide range of Subjects across all levels

Secondary Level

Students in this level can take at least eight classes depending on the number of selective subjects. The curriculum has changed over the years from the Junior Certificate to Checkpoint.

High School

The school offers the IGCSE and SGCSE Curriculum for students who are admitted to do Form 4 & Form 5.

A-Levels

Students at the Advanced Subsidary Level (Form 6L) can take at least four subjects, including English and Pure Mathematics which are compassory. In A-Level (Form 6U), the minimum number of subjects a student can take is three.
