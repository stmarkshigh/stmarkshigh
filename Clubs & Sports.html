Clubs & Sports

Inter-House Competitions

Information

Our school Inter-House competitions are held every year and they play a major role in the physical development of our children as everyone is expected to participate in an activity to help them learn valuable socialisation skills and the importance of team work.

The school offers several Inter-House activities and each activity has a Patron that acts as the head of each sport.

  • Shotput
  • Javelin
  • Relay Races
  • Long & Short Distance Running
  • High Jump
  • Long Jump

National School Competitions

Information

St Marks has always prided itself for its domination in several fields of competitvive sports around the country. We always try our best not only to win these school competitions bur also to have fun playing the sports we love. Our students are given enough time to explore different sports before they decide to settle on the one that they would like to compete in.

We offer several ball game sports as well as strategy oriented sports and other skill based activities for the children who want to be more on the classsy side of things.

  • Shotput
  • Javelin
  • Relay Races
  • Long & Short Distance Running
  • High Jump
  • Long Jump

Community Service

Information

We as an institution do our very best to help the community around us. Our school has two major clubs that that are envolved and responsible for coming up with projects that help provide food, clothing, textbooks and so on for the less priviledged communities around us. The Interact Club and The Environmental Club are the clubs that have over the years done a satisfactory job raising funds and giving out food parsels to several communities.
