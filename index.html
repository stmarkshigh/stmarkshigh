About St Marks High School:

History

St Mark's High School is one many schools located in the capital city of Eswatini, Mbabane.However St Mark's stands out from the rest because of our consistant academic excellence which tops the countries competing schools with each year that passes.

Campus

We at St Marks are responsible for the accomodation of more than 400 students in our bording hostels. Students are provided with nutritious food on a daily bases and have the freedom of using many of the schools state of the art facilities to help in make their learning alot easier.Some of these facilities include the 1.School Library and 2.Computer Lab ,to name a few.

Extracurricular activities

We take pride in knowing that when our students finish their High School years with us, they not only leave with excellent grades but they always leave having found an extra curricular activity which helps them as they venture out into this world full of millions of talented people. They can proudly say that they have not only achieved academic excellence, but they are able to persue their dreams as Actors,Athletes and so on.
